Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Its a version control software that allows us to collaborate with other developers in an easy environment. We can exchange/modify/upload code.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: For business analytics we can use data science to come up with meaningful insights on how a business is doing.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Rebasing will overwrite the history of all branches that are being merged while Merging preserves the history of each branch as a tree.
